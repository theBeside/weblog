Thinking to apply for a quick loan? If any of you is looking to get a low-rate loan in the US, then Lending Club is one of the best options available for you. It is peer-to-peer lending company and offers unsecured personal loans up to $40,000. Lending Club allows various online services to its borrowers. All they have to do is to log in at www.lendingclub.com. The complete procedure for Lending Club Login is discussed in this article.

Lending Club Login Process

In order to log in to your Lending Club online account, simply follow the given procedure:

  • First of all, turn on your PC and make sure that it is accessed with an internet connection.
  • Then, open an internet browser and go to lendingclub.com.
  • There you can see the option of “Sign-in” on the home page. Click on it.
  • It will ask you enter your account email address and password. After entering email address and password, you can choose to remember your email.
  • After that, click on the button “Sign-in” to finish this process.

Once you`re logged in to your account, you can get various online services. In case, any of you don’t remember your email password, then don’t worry. Click on the option “Forgot Password?” and complete the procedure to get access to your account back. So what are you waiting for? Just follow the given above Lending Club Login process.  But if you don’t have your account, then you have to create a new one first.

How to Create New Account

If you are a new user, then you have to create your own account first. Just follow the given steps:

  • Open an internet browser and go to www.lendingclub.com.
  • Click on the option “Sign-in”. There go to “Don’t have an account yet?”
  • Then select your state, account type, enter the email address and create a password.
  • Click on the button “Next” to verify your identity.
  • After verifying your identity, you can fund your account and choose an investment strategy as well.
Features of Online Account
After completing the Lending Club Login process, borrowers can get various online services. They can pay the bill online, get complete details of monthly payments and many more. So just log in to your Lending Club account online and manage your information with no difficulty.
